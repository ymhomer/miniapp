Rhythm Challenge

How to Play

Click the hit area or use arrow keys when blocks reach the bottom.

Normal Block: Click on the corresponding side for 1 point.
Reverse Block: Click on the opposite side for 2 points.
Trap Block: Don't click! It will pass safely.

Miss or wrong clicks cost one life. You have 3 lives. Good luck!
